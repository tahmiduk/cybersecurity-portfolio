# Information Security Policy — NexaTech Solutions

**Organisation:** NexaTech Solutions  
**Policy Owner:** Data Protection Officer  
**Prepared by:** Mohammad Ahnaf Tahmid  
**Date:** April 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Review Date:** April 2027  

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the information security requirements 
for NexaTech Solutions to protect customer data, ensure GDPR 
compliance and reduce risk to the organisation.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors and third 
parties who access NexaTech Solutions systems and data.

---

## 3. Access Control

- All systems must be protected with strong passwords
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all users
- Access rights must follow the principle of least privilege
- Access reviews must be conducted quarterly
- All access must be revoked immediately upon staff departure

---

## 4. Data Protection

- All customer personal data must be encrypted at rest and in transit
- Data must only be collected for specified, legitimate purposes
- Customer data must not be shared with third parties without consent
- Data retention periods must comply with UK GDPR requirements
- A Data Protection Officer (DPO) must be appointed

---

## 5. Incident Response

- All security incidents must be reported immediately to IT Security
- A data breach must be reported to the ICO within 72 hours
- All incidents must be logged and investigated
- Lessons learned must be documented after every incident

---

## 6. Patch Management

- Critical security patches must be applied within 48 hours
- All software must be kept up to date at all times
- Monthly vulnerability scans must be conducted
- An asset inventory must be maintained at all times

---

## 7. Staff Awareness

- All staff must complete security awareness training annually
- Phishing simulation exercises must be conducted quarterly
- New starters must complete security induction before system access

---

## 8. Business Continuity

- Critical data must be backed up daily
- Backups must be tested every 3 months
- A Business Continuity Plan must be maintained and reviewed annually

---

## 9. Compliance

- NexaTech Solutions must comply with UK GDPR at all times
- Annual GDPR audits must be conducted
- Non-compliance must be reported to senior management immediately

---

## 10. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or following any 
significant change to the business or regulatory environment.
